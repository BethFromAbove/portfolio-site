IONCLAD

Game Info

Background

Plasma cannons in space! How long can you survive?

How to Play

Use arrow keys to move cannon Hold Space to fire Destroy enemy ships before they reach your shield Collect friendly ships with your cannon to restore shield Press Enter to use a mega-bomb Manage your Power usage to avoid temporary outages
