Star Coat

This coat was my first attempt at some wearable tech and was a great introduction to the world of needles and conductive thread.

The coat shows the constellation Orion and has scientifically accurate star colours and placements. This doesn't show up too well in most of the stars, but Betelgeuse in the top left corner is showing off its Red Giant nature beautifully.
