Castle Plant Pot

My lovely trailing String of Hearts was looking rather sad coiled up on the floor of the windowsill, so I designed this Castle container to make it look like something out of a fairy tale.

The castle was designed in the style of an English medieval keep with crenellations on the walls and towers, printed and painted, before adding the clouds made of Polyester toy filling.

Hopefully my little String of Hearts will thrive now that it's dangling as it should be and I didn't even have to plant any magic beans to get this castle.
