Mission Impoppable

Mission Impoppable

Game Info

Background

They’re light on their feet with a colourful history and not afraid to go out with a bang. They’re the best damn agent in the field… but they need a little help getting around. You’ve been hired as their new handler. Can you guide them through their latest mission?

How to Play

Design and name your agent, then take them to the exit of each stage by clicking and dragging their name tag. Watch out for sharp objects!
